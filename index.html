About

I'm a PhD candidate at Cornell University, working with Dr. Perrine Pepiot. I work on computational combustion and computational fluid dynamics, with a particular focus on simulating realistic multi-component fuels. Projects have included developing chemical mechanism reduction algorithms, analysing fuel structural interactions, and characterising the role of multi-component fuels in spray evaporation. Most of the codes that I have worked on and written are not open source on my Github page, so please contact me if you desire more detail on the work that I have done.
